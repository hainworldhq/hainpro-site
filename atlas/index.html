Why this atlas matters

Hybrid AI-native systems do not rely on a single protocol. They emerge from a layered stack of standards for transport, identity, observability, tooling, agent collaboration, interaction, packaging, and governance.

HAINPRO organizes this stack so the missing execution and policy layer becomes easier to see.
